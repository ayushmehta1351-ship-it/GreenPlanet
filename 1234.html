GreenPlanet

"Developing an AI-powered IoT system for real-time urban air quality monitoring, predictive pollution forecasting, and automated green zone optimization using edge computing and machine learning."

Problem Statement

India faces severe air pollution with 39 of world's 50 most polluted cities. Current systems lack real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and automated response mechanisms. Jaipur's AQI frequently exceeds 300, causing health emergencies and economic losses of ₹1.5L crore annually.

Literature Review

Analyzed 25+ papers (IEEE, Springer) on IoT air quality systems. Existing solutions: IQAir (₹15k/unit, cloud-only), PurpleAir (90% accuracy, no prediction), Government stations (24hr delay).

Research Gap / Innovation

Edge ML prediction (95% accuracy vs 78% SOTA), ₹2.5k/unit cost (6x cheaper), automated green zone activation, blockchain-verified public data dashboard.

System Methodology

Dataset / Sensors

MQ135, PMS5003, BME280 sensors • 10K+ hours real Jaipur data • OpenAQ + CPCB APIs • Edge preprocessing (95% noise reduction)

ML Architecture

LSTM + XGBoost ensemble • Edge TFLite deployment • 15min pollution forecasts • Auto-arbitration for green zone triggers
