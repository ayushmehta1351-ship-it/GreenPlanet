How Carbon Credits Work

Understanding the lifecycle of a carbon transaction.

Carbon Credits

A unit of carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2e) that represents the removal or reduction of one tonne of greenhouse gas from the atmosphere.

Blockchain Security

Every transaction is immutably recorded on a distributed ledger, providing an unalterable record of ownership and transfer.

Transparency

All transactions are publicly auditable, ensuring that the integrity of carbon credit tracking is maintained.
